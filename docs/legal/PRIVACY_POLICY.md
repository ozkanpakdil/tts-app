# Privacy Policy for TTS App

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to TTS App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

## 2. Data We Collect
- **Identity Data**: Includes display name and email address provided through Google or Apple Sign-In.
- **Technical Data**: Includes Internet Protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, and other technology on the devices you use to access this app.
- **Usage Data**: Includes information about how you use our app, such as character count of text-to-speech requests and chosen providers.
- **Document Data**: We process text from PDF and TXT files you provide. This data is processed on-device (for free voices) or sent to our backend/cloud providers (for premium voices).

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We use your data to:
- Provide and manage your account.
- Authenticate your access to the app.
- Synchronize your preferences across devices.
- Process text-to-speech requests via our cloud providers.
- Track usage for billing and performance monitoring.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase (Google)**: For authentication and infrastructure.
- **Amazon Polly (AWS)**: For premium text-to-speech services.
- **Google Cloud TTS**: For premium text-to-speech services.
- **Azure Speech Services**: For premium text-to-speech services.

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy.

## 5. Data Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" button in the Profile section of the app. This will remove your identity data, preferences, and usage history from our servers.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@ttsapp.com.
